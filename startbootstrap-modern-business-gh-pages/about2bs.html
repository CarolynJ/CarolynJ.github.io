About Carolyn Jillson

I have always had a very practical approach to getting things done and enjoy thinking through solutions to complex problems. Some of the most rewarding and challenging work of my career has involved finding creative solutions to address tough questions.

  • As a marker researcher, I regularly evaluated emerging technologies and mobile platforms to determine the best data collection methods to meet our client's needs.
  • In grad school, I crafted a business plan outlining how city government agencies can collaborate to address childhood obesity on an environmental level.
  • As a Peace Corps volunteer, I discovered how malnourished children can be identified and how a community can work together to improve nutrition.
